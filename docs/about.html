Yesterday's News — About

⚠ Important Notice

This is a historical events app - NOT a breaking news application.

App Information

Yesterday's News is a historical events aggregator that summarizes notable events that occurred on this date in history across multiple languages. The app does not provide any political, financial, or emergency news coverage; it focuses exclusively on historical content.

Content Sources and Attribution

This app is a content aggregator. Content is compiled from publicly available historical sources, and each article's original source and publisher link is displayed in the app.

  • Each card contains a link to the original source.
  • Source and author information is prominently displayed when available.
  • Content is regularly updated at intervals; it is not static.
