Yesterday's News does not collect, store, or sell personal data. The app fetches public historical event and weather information to display on your device. Data We Access

Data Retention

Cached content is stored locally and automatically expires after a short period. You can clear the app cache at any time from your device settings.

Third Parties

The app may retrieve data from public APIs (e.g., Wikipedia/Open-Meteo). We do not send any personal data to these services.
